# Paper Revise

Help the author respond to reviewers strategically, politely, and completely.

## Workflow

1. Collect inputs: manuscript summary, full reviews, decision type, venue/journal rules, page/character limits, and non-negotiable author claims.
2. Build a comment table: reviewer, comment, category, severity, stance, response strategy, manuscript change.
3. Profile reviewers: positive, borderline, negative; expert or confused; constructive or dismissive; internally consistent or not.
4. Draft responses using: gratitude -> answer/ evidence -> exact manuscript change.
5. Ensure every promised change appears in the revised manuscript or change plan.
6. Use AC/AE messages only for factual errors, contradictions, extreme mismatch, or possible bias.

## Comment Categories

- Novelty / incremental contribution.
- Factual error or unclear description.
- Marginal improvement or unfair comparison.
- Missing experiments or ablations.
- Presentation, grammar, references, formatting.
- Scope or venue fit.
