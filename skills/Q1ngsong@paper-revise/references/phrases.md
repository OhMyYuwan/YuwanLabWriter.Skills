# Response Phrases

## Thanks

- We thank the reviewer for the constructive comment.
- We appreciate the reviewer raising this important point.
- This suggestion helped us improve the clarity of the manuscript.

## Agree and Fix

- We agree with the reviewer and have revised [Section X, Lines Y-Z].
- Thank you for pointing this out. We have corrected [issue] in the revised manuscript.
- We acknowledge that the original description was unclear and have rewritten it as follows.

## Clarify

- We would like to clarify that...
- The reviewer may have misunderstood our [claim/method/evaluation]. Our intended meaning is...
- To avoid this ambiguity, we have revised [location].

## Respectful Disagreement

- We respectfully disagree with the assessment that...
- We appreciate the concern; however, we believe the claim is justified because...
- While this is an important direction, it is outside the scope of the current paper because...

## New Evidence

- To address this concern, we conducted [experiment]. The results are shown in [Table/Figure].
- We have added [comparison/ablation/discussion] in [Section X].
- The results are consistent across [datasets/runs/settings], suggesting that...

## Location References

- revised manuscript, Sec. X, Lines Y-Z
- original submission, Line Y
- Table R1 / Figure R1 for rebuttal-only additions
- Table X / Figure X for material added to the revised manuscript

## Closing

- We hope this revision addresses the reviewer's concern.
- We believe the revised manuscript is clearer and stronger as a result of this suggestion.
