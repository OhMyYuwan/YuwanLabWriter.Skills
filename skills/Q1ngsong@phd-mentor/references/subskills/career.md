# Career Map

## Path Families

| Path | Evidence that matters | Preparation actions |
|---|---|---|
| Faculty / tenure-track | coherent agenda, strong papers, recommendation letters, job talk, teaching/service story | publish around a theme, give talks, draft research statement early, teach/TA intentionally |
| Postdoc | clear extension agenda, fit with host lab, publications or preprints, strong advisor support | contact 12-18 months early, prepare project pitch, clarify independence from PhD advisor |
| Industrial research | top papers or strong applied evidence, implementation skill, internship record, references | intern, open-source or demonstrate systems, translate research into product/research impact language |
| Engineering/product | robust coding/system/product skills, non-academic communication, internships | build portfolio, practice explaining value without PhD jargon |
| Research management/policy | organization, communication, field overview, cross-group credibility | organize seminars, write syntheses, practice stakeholder communication |
| Entrepreneurship | real problem, users/customers, team, technical boundary awareness | customer discovery, prototype, partner search, honest risk assessment |

## Network Capital

Network is not contact count. It is the set of people who can credibly speak for the user's work when the user is absent.

Build:

- Advisor letter.
- External collaborator or internship mentor letter.
- Community visibility: talks, workshop participation, reviews, questions at seminars.
- Peer network: today's peers become the field's middle layer.

## Overseas Visiting / CSC-Style Applications

Wang Guanghui's 2016 CSC experience is useful as a process example, not current policy.

Durable lessons:

- Best contact path: academic talk / face-to-face exchange -> email -> domestic advisor recommendation.
- Prepare English-language documents early.
- Funding, invitation, visa, travel, and arrival logistics are separate workstreams.
- Policies change; verify current CSC, host-university, embassy, and visa requirements.

## Grants and Independent Agenda

Grant writing is not paper writing. A proposal must persuade reviewers that the work is worth investing in.

Core ingredients:

- Important problem.
- Clear hypothesis or agenda.
- Preliminary evidence.
- Feasible plan.
- Independence from the PhD advisor's exact line of work.
- Fit with the funding program.
