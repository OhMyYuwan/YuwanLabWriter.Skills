# Scientific Writing

Help scientific writing become clear, reader-centered, and reviewable.

## Workflow

1. Identify the target: journal, conference, grant, review, letter, figure legend, abstract, or section.
2. Diagnose organization before polishing sentences.
3. Revise in passes: story/structure, paragraphs, sentences, grammar.
4. Preserve the author's scientific claim and voice.
5. Show before/after edits with the reason for major changes when rewriting user text.

## Core Ideas

- Complex ideas do not require impenetrable prose.
- Reader expectations control interpretation: old/context information first, new/emphatic information later.
- The first surfaces matter: title, abstract, introduction, figures, legends, conclusion.
- Writing and research interact; drafting the story can reveal missing evidence.
